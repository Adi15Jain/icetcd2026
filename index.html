About

About the Conference

The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Contemporary Dentistry (ICETCD 2026) is the premier global forum dedicated to the intersection of traditional clinical excellence and the rapid technological revolution in oral healthcare.

As we navigate an era defined by digital workflows, artificial intelligence, and personalized medicine, this conference serves as a vital compass for dental professionals.

Global Keynote Speakers

World-renowned dental experts sharing cutting-edge research and clinical innovations from across the globe.

Hands-on Workshops

Interactive learning sessions on digital dentistry, AI diagnostics, 3D printing, and regenerative techniques.

Paper & Poster Presentations

Present your research, compete for attractive cash prizes, and contribute to the evolving landscape of dentistry.

General Rules

  • Abstracts must be written in clear, publication-ready English and must not exceed 300 words.
  • Multiple submissions permitted; only one abstract per author will be selected for presentation.
  • Submissions must be original, unpublished, and aligned with the conference theme.
  • Abstracts previously published in journals will not be accepted.
  • A submission may include a maximum of two authors; only one shall be the designated presenter. Both authors receive a certificate of participation.
  • Any change of presenter must be formally communicated to the Organizing Committee.
  • No interchange between Online and Offline modes is permitted after confirmation.

Paper Presentation

10 min total 8 min presentation + 2 min Q&A
Offline

Bring presentation on a pen drive. Submit to registration desk / hall coordinator before the session. Presenter is responsible for pen drive safety.

Online

Share screen during live session. Ensure a professional background, stable internet connection, and clear audio quality.

Slide Structure (16:9 format · Non-serif font e.g. Arial)

1Title & Introduction
2Need for the Study, Hypothesis, Aims & Objectives
3Materials & Methods
4–6Results with Infographics & Tables
7Summary, Conclusions & Future Prospects
8Acknowledgements & References

Poster Presentation

5 min total 3 min presentation + 2 min Q&A
Offline

Bring file on a pen drive. Submit to registration desk / hall coordinator before the session. Presenter is responsible for pen drive safety.

Online

Share screen during live session. Ensure professional background, stable internet, and clear audio.

Poster Guidelines

  • Conducted entirely in digital format (PDF, PPT, or high-res JPEG).
  • Include: Introduction, Methods, Results, Conclusions, Recommendations & Acknowledgements.
  • Use short sentences, simple words, and bullets.
  • Self-explanatory graphics should dominate the poster.
  • Avoid jargon, acronyms, or unusual abbreviations.
  • Use a non-serif font (e.g., Arial).
  • Refer to drugs/devices by scientific names only — no trade names.

Conference Proceedings

Selected papers from Online and Offline Oral Presentations will be published in the Conference Proceedings.

  • Only selected papers will be considered — publication is not guaranteed.
  • Selection is at the sole discretion of the Scientific Committee.
  • The decision of the Scientific Committee is final.

Venue & Destination

Teerthanker Mahaveer University, Moradabad

Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) is a premier institution located in Delhi Road, Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. Spread over a sprawling campus, TMU is known for its world-class infrastructure, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to academic excellence. The university hosts numerous national and international conferences, making it an ideal venue for ICETCD 2026.
